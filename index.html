Email Verification & Data Cleanup for Soccer Clubs

A Sample Portfolio Deliverable

Project Overview

This sample demonstrates my ability to perform **detailed, manual data verification and enrichment** for a large dataset of U.S. soccer clubs. The objective was to eliminate stale contact information and provide actionable, verified general and key personnel emails from official sources.

My Meticulous 3-Step Process

Step 1

Research Website Contact Pages

Manually visit the official club domain to confirm existence and identify primary contact pages (About Us, Board, Staff Directory).

Step 2

Verify Email Accuracy

Use search operators and verification tools to validate the formatting and association of general (info@) and specific officer emails (president@).

Step 3

Record Verified Data & Notes

Data is meticulously recorded in the final sheet, along with an audit trail in the **Notes** column to provide proof of work and source verification.
